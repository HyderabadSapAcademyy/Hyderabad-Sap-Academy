SAP S4HANA FINANCE


SAP S/4HANA Finance, often abbreviated as S/4HANA Finance, is a financial management solution developed by SAP, a leading enterprise software company. It is part of the SAP S/4HANA suite, which is the next-generation enterprise resource planning (ERP) suite designed to run on the SAP HANA in-memory database. The "S/4HANA" in the name stands for "SAP Business Suite 4 SAP HANA."
